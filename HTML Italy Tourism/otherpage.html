Pros and Cons of Living In Italy


A List by Rohun Baxi


Pros:

  • Amazing Surroundings
  • A Buzzing Night Life

  • Good/Cheap Education
  • Well Maintained Housing Considering Its Age
  • Cheap and Tasty Food

  • Great Healthcare
  • Free-thinking and Sylish Locals
Italy's benefits come in its ability to maintain its ancient culture and beautiful
surroundings whilst still having modern thinking and infrastructure.

Cons:

  • Limited English Speakers
  • Dirty and Cramped Streets

  • Lack of Affordable Housing Options
  • Expensive Amenities and High Taxes
  • Mild Culture Shock

  • Dangerous Roads
  • High Unemployment With Logged Bureaucracy
Italy is significantly more crowded than Canada with high taxes and crowd levels. If you are not prepared, the culture shock and language barrier will sweep you away.
